## 使用说明 / How to Use

### 中文说明
1. 规划布局，测试灌木摆放位置
2. 在底部区域涂抹适量PVA胶或瞬间胶
3. 将灌木轻轻压入位置，保持片刻直至粘牢
4. 用草地、散料或静态草混合边缘
5. 可选：用稀释PVA或哑光喷剂密封

### English Instructions
1. Plan placement and test-fit the shrubs on your layout
2. Apply a small amount of PVA/white glue or super glue to the base area
3. Press the shrub gently into place; hold briefly until tacky
4. Blend edges with turf, scatter, or static grass
5. Optional: seal with diluted PVA or a matte spray

### 中文
- 保持干燥，避免阳光直射，远离热源以保持颜色和形状
