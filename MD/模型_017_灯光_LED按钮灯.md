## 产品描述 / Product Description

### 中文描述

**产品名称：** 塑壳按钮LED灯（3种光色可选）

**产品特点：**
塑壳按钮LED灯采用透明塑料外壳设计，灯板中心为按钮键，四周分布红、绿、蓝三个灯珠。支持三种闪光模式：快速闪、中速闪、慢速闪，通过按钮循环切换。自带可更换纽扣电池，快速闪模式下工作时长约48小时。尺寸为2.2cm直径×0.8cm厚度，净重仅约3克。提供暖光、冷光、彩灯三种版本，适用于沙盘模型、建筑景观、微缩场景等多种场合。

### English Description

**Product Name:** Plastic Button LED Light (3 Color Options)

**Product Highlights:**
This plastic button LED light features a transparent shell with a central button surrounded by red, green, and blue LED beads. Three flashing modes available: fast, medium, and slow, cycled via button press. Equipped with replaceable button battery, providing approximately 48 hours of fast flashing. Compact size: 2.2cm diameter × 0.8cm thickness, weighing only 3g. Available in Warm Light, Cool Light, and RGB Color versions. Ideal for dioramas, architectural models, and miniature scenes.
