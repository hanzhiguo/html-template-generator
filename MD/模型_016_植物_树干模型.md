## 产品外观描述 / Product Visual Description

可弯曲树干模型采用铁丝骨架搭配塑料涂层工艺制作，树干主体呈深棕色树皮纹理效果。产品最大特点是可任意弯曲塑形，用户可根据场景需求调整树枝角度和造型。根部配有不锈钢针，便于插入沙盘、地形粉等场景底座中固定。发货时可折叠收纳，不占空间。树干预留多个分支点，可配合任意颜色的灌木、树叶等配件进行装饰。

### English Description
The bendable tree trunk model is made with wire frame and plastic coating, featuring dark brown bark texture. Its key feature is the ability to be bent and shaped freely according to scene requirements. Equipped with stainless steel pins at the base for easy insertion into diorama bases. Foldable for compact shipping and storage. Multiple branch points allow decoration with any color shrubs or foliage accessories.

**产品特点：**

**Product Highlights:**
This bendable tree trunk model features a wire frame with plastic coating. Available in three sizes: Small (10cm height, 11cm diameter), Medium (15cm height, 14cm diameter), Large (17cm height, 16cm diameter). The trunks can be freely bent and shaped, with stainless steel pins at the base for secure fixation. Foldable design allows compact shipping and storage.
