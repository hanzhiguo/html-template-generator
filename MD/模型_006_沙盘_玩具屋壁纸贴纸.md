## 使用说明 / How to Use

### 中文说明
1. 清洁干燥待粘贴表面
2. 测量并标记需要粘贴的区域
3. 撕开背胶纸一角，对齐粘贴
4. 边贴边抚平，排除气泡
5. 用美工刀沿边缘切割多余部分
6. 如需更换，从角落轻轻撕起即可

### English Instructions
1. Clean and dry the surface
2. Measure and mark the area
3. Peel off a corner of backing paper, align and stick
4. Smooth out while sticking to remove air bubbles
5. Trim excess with craft knife
6. To replace, gently peel from corner
